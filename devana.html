Jane Doe

As a human, Alice was once known as the most beautiful woman in the kingdom and she became a duchess at 17 when she married into the royal family. Obsessed with her appearance and the praise it garnered, Alice became furious when younger and more beautiful women began to steal her spotlight. In a twisted quest to maintain her title as the most beautiful, she began plotting to kill them one by one. She invited them for tea in her chambers, and slipped poison into their cup. Her obsession with killing became too great to resist, and she continued to murder any woman who received even a modicum of praise for her beauty. Eventually, Alice was caught and sentenced to death for her crimes. She was beheaded in 1509. Her soul lived on as a demon in the demon world, where she remains vain and obsessed with beauty, targeting human women in her hunts.

Alice is a creature of haunting beauty and lethal cunning. With her long, slender horns and pale blue undertones, she dresses in the fashion of her 1500s noble upbringing. Despite her previous human life as a socialite, Alice now preys on other women, using her grotesque fangs and poison-laced nails to subdue and kill her victims. In her Oni demon form, her skin turns an eerie shade of pale blue with indigo veins, and her face contorts into a fearsome visage. Standing at a towering 6 feet tall, Alice's beauty belies her deadly nature, and those who underestimate her do so at their peril.
