Welcome to 22Studios!

Welcome to 22 Studios, an independent game development studio based in Sacramento! Our small team is dedicated to creating fun and innovative games for our community to enjoy. We're famous for our hit horror game Nefaryous, but we've also developed smaller games such as Battlesaga and Lulu. We appreciate our fans' support and are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all. Here on our website, you can find updates on our latest projects in our development blog, where we share insights on the game development process and behind-the-scenes content, or read our blog for industry insights and commentary. Get to know our team and their unique perspectives.

At 22 Studios, we believe that gaming should be a fun and inclusive experience for everyone. We strive to promote diversity and inclusion both in our games and within our team. We hope you enjoy exploring all that we have to offer!
